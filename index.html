Beyond competitions, I'm constantly engaged in personal projects and self-learning. With each endeavor, I strive to sharpen my skills and expand my knowledge in the world of low-level development and information security. Looking ahead, my aspirations include joining a team where I can continue learning while making a meaningful contribution.
